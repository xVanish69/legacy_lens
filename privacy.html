Privacy Policy for Legacy Lens

Last Updated: 19/05/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Legacy Lens. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Anonymous authentication data (no personal information required)
  • Saved quotes and reflections
  • Progress through legacy weeks
  • App preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage statistics
  • Performance data
  • Error logs

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing and maintaining the app
  • Improving user experience
  • Analyzing app performance
  • Debugging technical issues
  • Personalizing content

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • We use AsyncStorage for data persistence
  • No data is sent to our servers unless explicitly shared

4.2 Firebase Storage

  • Firebase is used for content delivery only
  • No personal data is stored in Firebase
  • Anonymous authentication is used for future sync capabilities

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Local encryption for stored data
  • Secure data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Request data modification

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
